Coronet Peak

Coronet Peak is the countries longest running and first! commercial ski mountain, it has been running since 1947. Coronet Peak has provided to the community and supported the community as they have been acknowledging the wants from the visitors and they have been listening! As this season the have added the knew Coronet Express chair, giving us a 6 seated chair and soon to be added gondola's! Up here we allow all kinds of activities ranging from the freezing cold winter activities such as shredding the slopes, sight seeing and sledding and much more! We also have followed into the summer activities such as biking and hiking which allow for the use of the mountain all year round which lets our customers and you! to be able to enjoy your stay all the time at any hour!

Coronet Peak is perfect for the trail riders which love to hit the freshly groomed trails and push for the most runs they can possibly do! We also hold the winter games which consist of the racers which love to hit the icey trails to get the best time the possibly can! During your stay up here at Coronet Peak, you are able to stay for the extravagant night skiing, which is specifically only on Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays! This is a blast with all your family and friends and we sure hope to see you up here!

As apart of our business we supply all of our visitors with free water and the opportunity to explore around our vicinity and go on all sorts of adventures. You can also check out our cafe which is found in our base building and our very own restaurant! Hope you have a great stay!
