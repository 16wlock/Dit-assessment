Coronet Peak

These are the local mountains within central otago which are popular tourist attractions and have many activities avaliable for you to do! These mountains are full of the winter activites such as, skiing and snowboarding, snow shoeing and even more during the winter time! Even throught the summer time the have many adventures avaliable such as mountain biking, hiking and even more!
