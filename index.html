Local Mountains

These are the local mountains within central otago which are popular tourist attractions and have many activities avaliable for you to do! These mountains are full of the winter activites such as, skiing and snowboarding, snow shoeing and even more during the winter time! Even throught the summer time the have many adventures avaliable such as mountain biking, hiking and even more!

This website has been created to display and showcase our significant mountain range within the Otago region in the wonderful South Island of New Zealand. These mountains are best for the thrill seeking adventurists of the globe. Absolutely everybody from every part of the globe is welcome to join us in seeking new adventures in our multiple playgrounds full of snow amongst a varse mountain range! We can provide tours to whoever it may be and will assist you through out your stay to make it as best as can be!

We provide all kinds of services as we offer free water in the base buildings at the ski resorts, in which we also have our own selection of wonderful food cooked and created up in our very own kitchens, which we would love for you to come have a try if your aren't really into the risk taking endeavours.

Our mountains are home to all differnt styles of snow sports. Such as the easy to access and glorious, Coronet peak which has the spectacular views of the Wakatipu basin and shows us the best time thorugh out the ski season presenting us with all types of skiing ranging from, the groomed trails, soft and fluffy off piste, all the way to the hard, fast and ice stuff! perfect sor the speedsters which love to go fast!
